Edgar Ramos

Growing up, I had many wants and desires. I wanted to be an artist, live the rockstar life style, popping bottles-- all that crazy stuff. nah! just kidding. I wanted to join the art institute in order to learn how to draw better. Drawing was my passion, but that dream didn't last long after I discovered Runescape. In order to play Runescape I needed a computer. Luckily for me, my parents had bought a brand new Toshiba laptop, so I snuck off with it at night to play this game all my friends were playing at the time. I wanted and needed a way for my character to level up since I wasn't putting in as much play time as my friends were. I descovered that there were programs you could download as a sort of plug in that would play for you as a bot (but Runescape hates bots). I remember watching a bunch of code just move along the side of this program and as the code moved, my avatar in the game would move too. Watching that code made me want to learn how to code. Finally after years of pushing that desire to learn down, I decided to learn by joining the UC Davis Full Stack Coding Boot Camp. For those who read this far, I have this for you.
